Chemical Engineering Students' Association

Chemical Engineering Students' Association is a student-centric fraternity which aims at the holistic development of the students of the Chemical Engineering Department of VIT, Pune. The association conducts various technical workshops, seminars, guest lectures, and an annual conference for the students to assimilate and gain knowledge in the field of Chemical Engineering and Sciences.

About Us

ChESA is a vibrant community of chemical engineering students dedicated to fostering academic excellence, professional development, and networking opportunities. Our association serves as a bridge between academic learning and industry exposure.
